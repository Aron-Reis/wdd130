Overview

Purpose

"This website serves as a vital tool for attracting and informing potential customers. It showcases the company's services, packages, and safety measures, providing essential details to adventurous individuals seeking thrilling experiences. Additionally, the website facilitates such as online bookings, customer reviews, and contact information, make it easier for customers to plan their trips. By enhancing visibility and accessibility, the website helps the company reach a broader audience and establishes a professional online presence. Thus the purpose of this website is to Build Revenue for this Rafting Company."

Audience

The target customers for the rafting company are adventure enthusiasts ranging from young adults to middle-aged individuals, typically aged 18-50, with a moderate to high disposable income. They are interested in outdoor activities, nature, and adrenaline-pumping experiences. These customers seek exciting and memorable adventures that provide a break from their routine lives. By accessing the website, they expect to find detailed information about rafting trips, safety protocols, pricing, and booking options. Their primary needs include clear and comprehensive details on the types of rafting experiences available, the safety measures in place, and the ease of making reservations. These needs are often unmet due to a lack of easily accessible, consolidated information. The audience will usually access the site through mobile devices for convenience, but also use desktops and laptops for more detailed planning and bookings.
